An R Users' Group in Davis

September 24, 2012

 
I'm excited to share that we've started a new R users' group at UC Davis! Right now our main purpose is to run weekly 2-hour work/hack sessions where R users can get together to work through problems together. More info [here](http://www.noamross.net/davis-r-users-group.html)
